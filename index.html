About Me.

With over 12 years of hands-on experience in Data Engineering, I am passionate about transforming complex data into meaningful insights that drive impactful business decisions. My journey in this field has been marked by building scalable, high-performance data pipelines, architecting data solutions that stand the test of scale, and ensuring data quality and governance across diverse platforms.

As a seasoned Data Engineer, I have had the privilege of working with cutting-edge technologies across cloud ecosystems, real-time processing frameworks, and advanced ETL tools. As this quote says, "Without data, you’re just another person with an opinion" — W. Edwards Deming. My work is dedicated to making data accessible, insightful, and actionable.

Throughout my career, I’ve led projects in areas such as big data, cloud migrations, data lakehouse architectures, and automated data quality checks. My core strengths include designing sustainable data infrastructures, implementing robust CI/CD practices, and delivering insights that align data strategy with organizational goals.

When I'm not optimizing pipelines or tuning databases, I enjoy mentoring aspiring data engineers, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and staying on top of industry trends to bring the latest innovations to my work.

Let's connect if you’re passionate about leveraging data to drive results!
